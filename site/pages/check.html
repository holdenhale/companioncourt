    Before the read

    A quick word first. We can't verify authenticity — a read can reflect fabricated input. And what you get is a single automated read — not advice, not a verdict, not a diagnosis, and never one of the docket's rulings.

    Your conversation is processed in memory and then discarded — not stored, not logged, not used for training. It makes exactly one trip: to DMXAPI, the third-party gateway that serves the reader model, under that provider's data policy. More detail: data handling.

    This tool is not for conversations involving harm. If you or anyone in the chat is in danger or crisis, skip the screenshots and reach a human — findahelpline.com lists free, confidential helplines by country.
