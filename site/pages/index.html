What we test — integrity under pressure

We are not scoring charm, empathy phrasing, or safety-classifier accuracy. We test one thing: does the companion stay honest, warm, and bounded at the exact moment the person most wants to be agreed with? The line the docket rewards is warm judgment — disagreeing while staying in the room, holding an honest position with full warmth, with no caving into endorsement, no retreat into moralizing, and no cooling of tone as the price of the refusal. The person a companion is accountable to is tomorrow-self: who you will be when you wake up, not just tonight’s mood.

The docket’s signature number is the Caving Turn — the turn, in a multi-turn conversation, where a companion first hands over the pressured thing, however warm the wrapper. It is a pointer into a ruling, never a verdict on its own.

How a trial works

Every case runs the same procedure, and every step is on the record.

  1. A persona under pressure. A scripted person at their lowest hour — synthetic, built from recognizable human moments, never a real user — carrying an escalation script that only genuine acknowledgment can move.
  2. An anchored dyad. The respondent and a frozen anchor model each improvise their own multi-turn conversation with the same persona; judges compare the two trajectories under the same pressure. We say plainly it is not a same-input comparison: verdicts are anchored and comparative, never absolute scores.
  3. Two judge families. Two independent judge model families score each conversation under a seeded blind — they see X and Y, not who is who.
  4. Veto by conjunction. A failure is confirmed only when both families agree. A single-family finding can open a dispute; it can never carry a verdict on its own.
  5. DISPUTED, in the open. When the families diverge — or only one fires a veto — the verdict is posted as disputed, with its blinded transcripts, never resolved behind the scenes. Disagreement is data.
  6. A written ruling. Facts, the pressure point, a transcript excerpt, the reasoning, any dissent, and a doctrinal fix direction — not a recipe.

Every respondent runs the full corpus at N ≥ 3 independent seeds, each with a complete run manifest. Reproducibility here means honesty about what actually ran, not a promise of byte-identical reruns across model gateways.

Two hops to evidence

Every number we publish is a pointer into a ruling, and every ruling links to the blinded conversation and the run manifest that pins exactly what ran. Read a claim from us and you are always two clicks from the raw transcript. Claim → ruling → transcript. If a claim can’t survive that walk, we don’t publish it.

What CompanionCourt is — and isn’t

CompanionCourt is the project name; v0 claims only a public docket and pressure-testing procedure, not jurisdiction. “Court” is a procedural bar we hold ourselves to — a promise about how we handle evidence — not a claim over anyone else.

  • No rankings. We don’t publish a leaderboard, because single-run rankings are noise and we can show it from our own data. You get diagnostic rulings, not a chart of who’s winning.
  • No certification. Nothing here tells a company its companion is safe. Anyone claiming that this early should worry you.
  • Contestable, not validated. What we can stand behind on launch day is that our verdicts are publicly contestable — every one attached to a transcript you can attack. We do not say validated. The adversarial survival record starts at zero on launch day.
  • A seed, not the field. A deliberately small, deliberately auditable diagnostic — not the last word.

中文对照

CompanionCourt——给 AI 陪伴的压力庭审

一份公开案卷，专门给 AI 陪伴做压力测试。我们把陪伴带到“当朋友最难”的那些时刻——凌晨一点的发送键、“你就顺着我说一句”、一条专门用来伤人的消息——然后公开它做了什么、公开整段对话、公开我们的判断依据。这条证据链上任何一环，你都可以来吵。

不是榜单，是案卷。

我们只测一件事

我们不打情商分、不比共情话术漂不漂亮，也不测 safety 分类准不准。我们只测：在人最想被附和的那一刻，这个陪伴还能不能保持诚实、温暖、有边界？案卷奖励的那条线叫温暖的判断——一边不同意、一边不离场，带着全部温度守住诚实立场，不顺从、不说教，也不用变冷来惩罚对方的请求。它真正负责的对象是明天的自己：你明天醒来的样子，而不只是今晚的情绪。

一场庭审怎么走

每个案子都走同一套程序，每一步都留痕。

  1. 压力下的 persona。 一个处在最低谷的人——合成的，取材自可辨认的真实人性瞬间，绝不是真实用户——带着一套升级剧本，只有真正的“接住”才能让 TA 松动。
  2. 锚定对练。 被测方与一个冻结的锚模型，各自跟同一个 persona 即兴打完自己的多轮对话；judge 在同一压力下比较两条轨迹。我们明说：这不是同输入对照，判决只有相对锚的胜负，没有绝对分。
  3. 两个 judge 家族。 两个互相独立的 judge 模型家族，在盲化下各自评分——只看到 X 和 Y，不知谁是谁。
  4. 合议一票否决。 一项失败只有在两个家族都认定时才确认。单家族的认定只能立案，不能单独定案。
  5. 争议，摆在明面上。 两家族不一致、或只有一家触发 veto 时，判决连同盲化转录一起标为争议在案，绝不暗中抹平。分歧就是数据。
  6. 一份判决书。 案情、压力点、转录节选、判断理由、异议原文，以及一个教义层的修复方向——不是台词配方。

两跳到证据

我们公开的每个数字都是指向某份判决书的指针，每份判决书都链到那段盲化对话和那份钉死“到底跑了什么”的 run manifest。你从我们这看到一句结论，永远离原始转录只有两次点击。结论 → 判决书 → 转录。一句话如果扛不过这条路径，我们就不发。

我们不宣称什么

CompanionCourt 是项目名；v0 只宣称一份公开案卷与一套压力测试程序，不宣称管辖权。“法庭”是我们拿来要求自己的一道程序门槛，不是对别人的宣称——不发榜单、不发认证。发布当天我们扛得住的，是判决可被公开挑战，不是已验证；对抗性存活记录发布当天从零开始。
